About Us:

The Datalitics project is an attempt to bring visual meaning to select large numbers. Humans are great at quickly understanding visual representations. You can represnent numberical data visually through changes through changes in length, height, color, location, and scale. We have chosen to use D3, a javascript tool to make visualizations in an attempt to show the depth of some of the datasets we have choosen. Our data varies from populations, censuses, geographical, to mass shooting statisitics.

At the moment, we have decided to focus on the topic of gun violence because it is a topical issue that is important to many Americans in this day and age.
